About This Game

🐾 PAW Patrol Math Rescue!

An Educational Integer Math Game

Original Series Creator

PAW Patrol was created by Keith Chapman, a British television writer and producer. PAW Patrol first aired in 2013 on Nickelodeon and has become one of the most beloved and popular animated series worldwide.

🎓 Educational Goals

This game teaches integer operations through rescue missions:

➕ Addition ➖ Subtraction ✖ Multiplication ➗ Division 🔢 Negative Numbers 📊 Multi-step Problems

🎮 Game Features

  • 6 Rescue Missions — one for each original pup
  • 3 Difficulty Levels: Easy, Medium, Hard
  • Choose your pup leader for each mission
  • Ryder announces every mission
  • 3 Lives per game — lose all = Game Over
  • Hints appear after wrong answers

📖 How to Play

1️⃣Choose a difficulty on the main menu and press Start Rescue!
2️⃣Ryder will announce the mission. Pick a pup to lead it!
3️⃣Read the mission story then press Begin Mission!
4️⃣Solve each integer problem. Type your answer and press Check or Enter.
✅Correct → earn points, move forward!
❌Wrong → lose a ❤️ life. A hint appears to help you.
❤️You have 3 lives. Lose them all = Game Over!
◀Use the back arrow anytime to return to the main menu.
🏁Complete all 6 missions to save Adventure Bay!

🔢 Integer Rules Quick Reference

  • (+) + (+) = positive: 3 + 4 = 7
  • (−) + (−) = negative: −3 + (−4) = −7
  • (+) − (−) = add: 5 − (−2) = 7
  • (−) × (−) = positive: −3 × −2 = 6
  • (+) × (−) = negative: 4 × −3 = −12
  • Same signs ÷ = positive; Different signs ÷ = negative
